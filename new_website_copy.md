### Commercial Litigation

**Short description (card):** Strategic dispute resolution for high-stakes commercial conflicts.

**Full description (detail page):**
When a commercial dispute arises — a breach of contract, an unpaid debt, a partnership breakdown — the decisions you make in the first days matter. We approach every matter with a strategy-first mindset, focused on your risk, your timeline, and the commercial outcome you need.

We represent businesses in the High Court and Magistrates' Court, and advise throughout the dispute lifecycle — from early negotiation through to judgment and enforcement.

**Services:**
- Breach of contract disputes
- Debt recovery and enforcement
- Urgent interdict applications
- High Court and Magistrates' Court litigation
- Settlement negotiation and mediation strategy

### Employment & Labour Law

**Short description (card):** Employer-focused labour law guidance for compliance and dispute readiness.

**Full description (detail page):**
South African employment law is detailed, constantly evolving, and unforgiving of procedural errors. Whether you're managing a disciplinary matter, navigating a retrenchment, or defending a CCMA dispute, the outcome often depends on whether the right processes were followed from the start.

We help employers build solid employment frameworks and respond effectively when disputes arise.

**Services:**
- Employment contract drafting
- Disciplinary and grievance process support
- CCMA dispute representation support
- Workplace policy frameworks
- Retrenchment and restructuring guidance

**Services:**

We act as a reliable compliance partner, handling the filings, records, and documentation that keep your business properly constituted and audit-ready.
