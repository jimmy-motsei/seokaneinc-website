## Aesthetic Direction

- **Chosen direction:** Bold / Confident with Editorial overtones
- **Design reference:** Lexor — dark navy base, amber accents, sharp typography, generous whitespace, grid-breaking layouts
- **Rationale:** Legal + corporate credibility requires authority signals; City's executive background supports a confident, non-generic aesthetic; SA corporate buyers respond to precision and seriousness, not warmth-led design
- **Design implications:**
  - No rounded corners on cards (border-radius: 0 throughout — see `.card-base`, `.btn-primary`)
  - Amber is reserved strictly for CTAs and highlights — never decorative fill
  - Typography is Manrope 800 weight for all headings with tight tracking (`letter-spacing: -0.03em`)
  - Hero uses split layout: text left, portrait right, bleeding to viewport edge
  - Section alternation: white → `var(--color-surface)` → `var(--color-navy-dark)` for rhythm
- **Reference sites:** Lexor template (design inspiration only; not a copy)
